# Debt Crush

## Crush Debt. Build Freedom.

Debt Crush is the simple, motivating debt payoff planner designed to help you become debt-free faster.

Stop guessing which debt to pay first. Stop juggling spreadsheets. Stop wondering when you'll finally be free from debt.

Debt Crush creates a personalized payoff plan that shows exactly how to eliminate your debt, how much interest you'll save, and the fastest path to financial freedom.

---

## See Your Debt-Free Future

Whether you're paying off credit cards, personal loans, student loans, or car loans, Debt Crush gives you a clear roadmap forward.

Track all your debts in one place and instantly see:

- Your total debt balance
- Monthly payment obligations
- Estimated interest remaining
- Your projected debt-free date
- Progress toward your financial goals

Know exactly where you stand and where you're headed.

## Choose the Right Payoff Strategy

### Debt Snowball

Build momentum by paying off your smallest balances first.

Celebrate quick wins, stay motivated, and watch your debts disappear one by one.

### Debt Avalanche

Save the most money by targeting high-interest debt first.

Reduce interest costs and accelerate your path to becoming debt-free.

Compare both strategies and choose the approach that works best for your goals.
